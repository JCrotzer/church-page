Welcome to First Baptist Youth!

First Baptist Youth is a ministry for 6th-12th grade. Our purpose is to introduce youth to Jesus Christ, equip them with a faith that works, and to live life on a mission for Jesus to go out into the mission field to share the gospel. The teen years can be hard enough without overcomplicating student ministry. We strive to express three core truths to each teenage who attends with us.

1

Unfortunately, the world promises love based on performance, looks and likes for acceptance. But not God. He loves you the way you are and we dive deep into the Bible as a reminder of that. Because God really does love you.

2

Life can be crazy and confusing for students and we teach that God has a plan and purpose for each individual. It is the perfect plan. It is what we were called to do.

3

We believe life is better when it involves community with others. A community where students can grow together and encourage one another on this journey to grow in their relationship with Christ.
