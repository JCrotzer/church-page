The Journey
Starts Here

Everyone has a next step. What's Yours?

We hope our church can be a place where you connect to others and find a home. Here at our church, we believe in the core values of inclusivity and redemption, grace and love, and the transforming power of Christ. We are all committed to walking the journey together. Whether you're single, married, with or without children, young or old--we invite you to experience life and growing with us at First Baptist Church of Spring Hill.

We're so excited you're interested in learning more about who we are and joining the family!

Your Journey Begins
Here

We welcome you to stop by the welcome desk located in the lobby of the main entrance. Our volunteers can help you find information and get connected! We look forward to meeting you!

Connect with small groups

Small groups are great for finding support, comfort and building close friendships with others. In small groups, you will find a place to pray, talk about life, laugh, hear others and their journey with Christ and even minister to others. Learn and grow through
Classes

Here at First Baptist, we provide many different classes, so you can connect with others, learn more and grow as a believer. We provide classes for all ages at 8:45am on Sunday mornings. Belonging to a class helps you connect with other believers who can be with you along the journey.
