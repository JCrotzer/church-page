My First Baptist
Account

My First Baptist is your access to things you need quickly. Church Life is a web portal to our church database. It provides members with 24/7 access to their own profile information so they can update their records, connect with other members, manage your online giving and so much more!

Download for Iphone Download for Android For other devices

***You must first setup an account and log in at least once from the website before you will be able to use the ChurchLife mobile app.

Create a Church Life Account Already have a Church Life Account

What can you do with Church Life?

  • Update your information(address, email, phone number)

  • Check your records to make sure what we have on file is correct.

  • Find the church directory so you can connect with others

  • Give online: This is a convenient and secure way to give.

How do I sign up?

  • First, create a Church Life account by clicking on the link above.

  • Church Life will verify you are in the church database and send you an email with a username and startup password. If you encounter problems, email the church office at info@fbspringhill.com to update your email address so you can log in.

  • Use the username and password from the email to log in and create a permanent password. You will now be able to login and use Church Life anytime.

This is a wonderful tool to help how we communicate to other members!
