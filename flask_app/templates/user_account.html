My First Baptist
Account

My First Baptist Account is where you can access information quickly. You can update your records, connect with other members, manage your online giving and so much more!

Create a FB account Login to my FB account

What can you do with FB Account?

  • Update your information(address, email, phone number)

  • Check your records to make sure what we have on file is correct.

  • Find the church directory so you can connect with others

  • Give online: Download the Church App for a convenient and secure way to give.

How do I sign up?

  • Create an account by clicking on the link above.

  • We will verify you are in the church database. If you encounter problems, email the church office at info@fbspringhill.com to update your email address so you can log in.

This is a wonderful tool to help how we communicate to other members!
