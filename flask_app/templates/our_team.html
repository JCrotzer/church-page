Meet Our Team

JC Christian, Senior Pastor

brojcc@gmail.com

Brother J was born in San Angelo TX. He is married to Patty Kilgore Christian. They have one daughter, Brook, who along with her husband Jason, have blessed Bro. J and Patty with five grandchildren.

Bro J has a Bachelor of Ministry (Cum Laude) from Covington Theological Seminary, a Bachelor of Arts in Religion (Magna Cum Laude) from Belmont University and a Master of Theology from Campbellsville University.

His hobbies include golf and riding his Harley.

Danny Brashier, Student Pastor

danny@fbspringhill.com

Danny has a BA in Biblical Studies from Blue Mountain College and a Masters of Arts in Christian Education from Mid-America Baptist Theological Seminary. He has been serving in student ministry since 1999, when he felt the calling of God on his life to surrender to full time ministry. Danny is married to Rebekah, and they have three amazing boys: Joshua, Caleb, and Samuel. In his free time, Danny enjoys collecting vinyl records and baseball cards.

Jonathan Lingle, Worship Leader

jonathan@fbspringhill.com

Jonathan has a BS in Commercial Music from Greenville College and Master of Music from Illinois State University (Emphasis in Piano Performance and Music Composition. Jonathan plays, performs, and records on over 20 instruments. Jonathan is married to Billie and they have three daughters: Sadie, Sofie, and a baby girl Cadence.

Paula Zimmer, Ministry Assistant

info@fbspringhill.com

Paula joined the staff of First Baptist in October 2012 bringing over 20 years of church office experience. Paula and her husband, John, love riding their motorcycle and are active members of FAITH Riders Motorcycle Ministry. They have three adult children (all married), two grandsons, and two granddaughters.
